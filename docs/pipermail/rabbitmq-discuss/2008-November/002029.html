Ilya,

Are you using Linux? If so, did the Linux OOM killer maybe zap the Erlang
node?

You could try this:

$ sudo sysctl vm.overcommit_memory=2
vm.overcommit_memory = 2

Also take a look at overcommit_ratio and swappiness.

HTH.
Ed
