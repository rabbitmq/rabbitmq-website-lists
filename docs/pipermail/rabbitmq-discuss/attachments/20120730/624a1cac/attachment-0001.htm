    Firstly, it&#39;s important to point out that letting the Channel
    process mailbox fill up could lead to unexpected and unpredictable
    performance characteristics, and doesn&#39;t appear to offer anything
    that per-connection flow control isn&#39;t already doing. Also, creating

    certainly) not the one you intended.<br>
