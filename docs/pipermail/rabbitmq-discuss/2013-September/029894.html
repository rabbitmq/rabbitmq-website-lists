According to the changelogs (http://www.rabbitmq.com/changelog.html) policy
based configuration of mirrored queues didn't come out until version 3.0.  I
believe before then the mirroring policy had to be declared when the queue
was created.  Eager synchronization (sync_queue) wasn't released until
version 3.1.0.
